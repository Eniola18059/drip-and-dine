Common Questions

You asked, we answered.

Do I need to download any app?
No. You only need WhatsApp, which you almost certainly already have. There's no Drip n Dine app to download — everything happens in your existing WhatsApp.
Do you deliver to my area in Lagos?
We deliver across Lagos. When you send your order via WhatsApp, we'll confirm your exact delivery area and give you the full details during your chat.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by location. We'll give you an estimated time when we confirm your order on WhatsApp. We move as fast as we can — always.
Can I customise my order?
Yes. Just let us know any preferences or substitutions in your WhatsApp message after the pre-filled order sends. We'll do our best to accommodate you.
How do I pay?
Payment details are shared in your WhatsApp chat after we confirm your order. We'll walk you through everything — no surprises.
Can I call instead of using WhatsApp?
Yes, you can reach us on 0906 427 3679. However WhatsApp is the fastest way to order as your message arrives pre-filled and we can process it immediately.
