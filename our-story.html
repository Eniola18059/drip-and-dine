The Origin

It started with hunger — and not just for food.

Drip n Dine was built by people who believed the Lagos food scene deserved something different. Not another spot doing the same thing. A place with a real identity — food that hits, a name you remember, and a vibe that makes you feel like the main character every single time you order.

We started with a simple question: what if the food was as bold as the people eating it? The answer is what you're looking at right now. A menu built from scratch, combining the familiarity of suya with global street food formats like Cheesesteak and Breadwarma, tied together with Boba Tea and Milkshakes that bring it all home.

Every item on this menu was deliberate. Nothing made the cut by accident.

The Founders

Two friends. One bold idea.

Drip n Dine didn't start in a boardroom — it started between two friends, Anjola and Goodness, who shared the same frustration: Lagos deserved a food brand with real character. Not just good food, but a whole identity you could feel from the first bite.

"We weren't trying to build a restaurant. We were trying to build something people talk about — a brand that feels like Lagos at its boldest."

— Anjola & Goodness, Co-Founders

Together they built the menu from scratch — fusing the boldness of suya with global street food formats, curating a Boba Tea lineup that actually makes sense, and designing combo deals that keep people coming back. Every decision was deliberate. Every item earned its place.

What started as a shared vision between two friends is now a brand that carries their energy in every order.
